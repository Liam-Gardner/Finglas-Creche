Your Child's Day

“Help me be independent!” We strongly believe in this quote that’s why we created this beautiful place, a place where children feel important and loved, a place where they can learn strong and lifelong skills, a place they can surely call a second home.

Babies

We understand that sometimes, starting your baby in a crèche can be a difficult time for parents. Bearing this in mind, our baby rooms are designed to be warm and welcoming and our highly trained professional carers will provide you with the much needed support during this transition. Our soft furnishings in the baby room and warm soothing atmosphere Read more... in the sleeping room will create a home from home environment for your baby. In our baby rooms our staff will follow the routine already established by you for your baby. We will follow your baby’s routine for eating and sleeping to ensure that your baby gets exactly what she/he needs throughout the day. Staff will carefully monitor your baby while they sleep in our separate cot room. In all our baby rooms you will find equipment to stimulate your baby’s senses such as musical instruments and toys of differing texture. We sing songs and read stories on an everyday basis and offer lots of cuddles throughout the day. We provide a carefully chosen nutritious menu for growing babies that includes a hot lunch and morning and afternoon snacks. For our smaller babies we offer bottles throughout the day in the arms of one of our loving carers.

Wobblers

As a wobbler, your child takes their first steps and really begins to explore the world around them. We help with activities that develop your child’s balance and motor skills, they will learn through art, water and messy play, dressing up, garden time, singing, dancing. All our fun activities also help to develop your child’s communication and encourage language Read more... development. Near the end of this stage in your child’s life toilet training is a big milestone. We allow the child to take the lead with this process and watch out for signs and language to show us that he/she is ready for this stage. We will at all times keep parents informed of their child’s progress. The staff has lots of experience in this area and are on hand to guide you through it!

Toddlers

In this age group children reach many milestones and their development is rapid. We therefore offer a well-rounded programme with a wide variety of play areas to stimulate your child’s senses and challenge their capabilities. In our bright inviting and active rooms your child’s language and vocabulary will develop, they will start to play with other children and make lots of new friends.Your child’s concentration will increase and Read more... they will spend longer at completing tasks. The staff will sing songs and read stories to your child. Our outdoor areas are all well-equipped, developmentally challenging and most importantly fun areas. The children go outside to play regularly throughout the day. We provide healthy and nutritious hot lunch and snacks for your child. The children are all encouraged to eat together with our staff which helps to promote good table manners and social interaction with each other. We provide vegetarian menus and any allergies a child might have will be taken into account when preparing their meals. Water and milk are available throughout the day and at mealtimes. Our staff will gently soothe your child to sleep on their own sleep mat with soft music for a nap daily.

Afterschool Care

Our after school care includes a diverse programme which allows children to thrive in a stimulating yet warm and welcoming environment. Our relaxed, yet carefully designed curriculum will meet the needs of each individual child. We will collect your child from local schools. Your child will be involved in designing activity plans. Our outdoor areas will ensure your child gets the opportunity Read more... to partake in various outdoors activities everyday. Our activities will be centred around areas such as cooking, arts and crafts, music, dance and drama, indoor/outdoor sports, group and individual games, reading, dress up and imaginary play We will provide you with a full report of your child’s day every day verbally and in written form should you require. We can also offer childcare for you on mid-term breaks, summer holidays and any other scheduled days off school.
