Welcome to Finglas Crèche

Welcome to Finglas Creche & Day Care At Finglas Creche & Day Care we believe that every child is special, that's why we pride ourselves in taking extra special care of your little one, We aim to provide a safe,secure environment from infant to school age.Your little one will learn through a combination of play, creative and structured. Children aged 2 months to 5 years and our After school Children can avail of our full-time or part-time service ,offering great nutrition and early learning programmes for all ages. We promise you the highest standards of care for your child, while offering you excellent value for money. By choosing Finglas Creche & Day care you have the peace of mind that comes from knowing that a highly competent, qualified and warm team are caring for your child. Our centre is a modern, purpose-built facility, designed specifically for children and built to the highest standards of safety and security. We have incorporated extensive security and safety features in the design of our centre and have rigorous safety procedures in place, to ensure that your child is kept safe in our care at all times.

From organised group play to individual activities, your child is constantly encouraged to grow as an independent person and interact in a group. You’re always welcome at Finglas Creche and Day care, we operate an open door policy and encourage parents to drop in anytime to spend time with us. We strive to provide as flexible a service as possible, continuously consulting with you to provide an excellent early childhood care and education experience. Above all, we aim to provide a service which is a ‘home away from home’ for your daughter or son. Our attendance options are extremely flexible and we are willing to work with our parents to meet their needs. We also provide a school collection service for your convenience. We will then care for your child in our Afterschool club for the afternoon, providing hot nutritious dinners as well as healthy snacks in an environment which is warm and homely as well as developmentally stimulating.
