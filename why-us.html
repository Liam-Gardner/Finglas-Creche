Why us

We know that finding the right childcare can be difficult, That’s why all our staff have all the necessary qualifications and experience and they strive to provide a loving and stable environment that children feel secure and protected in. We want learning and education to be fun. Our staff have consistent classroom presence to guide each child on their amazing path of discovery.

The strong and healthy Montessori system

Finglas Crèche is an educational centre built on the strong and healthy foundation of the Montessori System. We strongly believe that an authentic Montessori crèche should always keep in mind not only the correct way of implementing the method but equally, the love and care needed while teaching the child. Read more... We understand the importance of the accommodation period, both for parents and the child, that’s why you can count on us when we offer a healthy transition process; this helps you keep your mind at ease and the child to prevent separation anxiety.

Our "by the book" curriculum

The principles of Montessori education are obvious after your first step into the crèche ; large spaces, natural light, warm colours, contribute to a safe and secure medium that aims to bring out peace and focus for the children. Our curriculum is based on: Practical life, Sensory development, Communication development, Science and culture, Mathematics, Optional activities

Great outdoor activities

We do not limit ourselves or the children to indoor activities, we pride ourselves with the outdoor spaces as well. We completely understand the need for them to be surrounded by nature, so that as often as weather let’s us we organize outdoor activities in our beautiful garden or the playground. Children are able to interact with rabbits or parrots so they can better understand respect and empathy for animals. We just launched a new programme, Read more... a live garden where they work towards helping to grow vegetables, and in the end fill their heart with joy and proudness of achieving a great scope by consuming what they’ve grown.

Mens sana in corpore sano

Healthy nutrition and regular exercises is an important part of each of our lives, that’s exactly why we are getting them used to these principles of healthy eating and properly taking care of their bodies through regular exercises that in time become a habit or a routine. We have BabyYoga and KidsGym classes every day.
