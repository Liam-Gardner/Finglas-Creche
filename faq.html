FAQ's

Our crèche & Day care opens from 7.30am – 6.30pm Monday - Friday Please contact us for more information.

Our caring childcare professionals are Garda vetted and hold a minimum of FETAC level 5. In addition we operate an internal training programme to ensure that all our staff is fully trained to support children at all stages of their development.

All our staffs are trained in paediatric first aid so we are equipped to respond correctly in any situation. Refresher training takes place annually.

Yes we offer a free settling in period of one week to help parents and babies/children to adjust to their new routine.

Yes our crèche has a safe and secure outdoor play area with a selection of play equipment to enjoy. We have recently updated our gardens to provide more imaginative play. These materials include open sand and gravel pits and water stations.

Yes we have an open door policy where parents can pop in at any time of the day.

Fees are calculated per calendar month, paid monthly in advance, and are payable by the first of the month.

We are open during Halloween, summer and Easter breaks. We close for public bank holidays, Good Friday and for a period over Christmas.
