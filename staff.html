Our Staff

Our child workers have child psychology studies, two of them with Masters Degree and strong national and international work experience in this area. The other two have full IMEB(Irish Montessori Education Board) accreditation. The only thing all of them have in common is the love for children.

Jane Doe

My name is Jane; I’m 30 years old, mother of 3 happy kids. My mission here is to make sure make sure all the layers of education we implement here (intellectual, aesthetics, physical, moral-civic, ecologic, empathic and hygienic) are in complete harmony. This ultimately helps children to develop autonomous qualities to adapt to new social and educational challenges. The basis for Read more... my work is mutual respect and tolerance for each other; this enables our children to see each other as a part of an inter-cultural society, THE WHOLE WORLD.

Julia Doe

Hi, I’m Julia, 27 years old and in love with children and children psychology. Since I graduated 4 years ago I’ve been working in this field. My vision is that in life some of the most important things are relations and interaction between people. This is the reason my goal is to provide the crèche with spaces to create and develop relationships. In our mixt age and intercultural groups free play is a must. Read more... During this free play sessions, kids learn to interact, communicate, they learn how to respect and tolerate each other and how to solve conflicts.This helps them later in life to develop fully independent relationships and interactions.

John Doe

As a parent myself I learned that not everything that I read and not everything that sounds good in theory applies to every child. Although children’s needs are the same, each one is different and the needs don’t manifest the same. With that in mind my approach is tailored for each and every one of them. I strongly believe that it’s down to us and the parents which path Read more... the kids follow in their life, so it is our responsibility to make sure it’s the best one!

Maria Doe

Hi there, my name is Maria and I’m Montessori enthusiast. I have a strong background in the Montessori system, child psychology and non-violent communication. Montessori Method is based on the premise that each child is equipped with everything he needs to develop a healthy growth, that’s why education needs to be in-sync with all aspects of his life. Measuring a child’s Read more... development through standard educational systems is not accurate because these systems ignore the sensibilities and particularities of each child. On the other hand, the Montessori Method allows us to teach each child in his own rhythm through the joy of discovering and experimenting new things. Although at times it may seem as just child-play, this helps in the adaptation process and in introducing to abstract concepts.
